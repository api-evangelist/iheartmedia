“Bill and Giuliana: The Podcast” Debuts Today Listen to the premiere episode now HERE LOS ANGELES – May 12, 2026 – iHeartPodcasts, the No. 1 podcast publisher globally according to Podtrac, today announced “ Bill and Giuliana: The Podcast ,” a candid new series from longtime television personalities and entrepreneurs, Bill and Giuliana Rancic. Blending humor, honesty, and lived experience, the duo brings refreshingly real conversations to the podcast space. Listen to the premiere episode now available HERE . Each week, “Bill and Giuliana: The Podcast” will dive into some of the internet’s most searched and often unspoken questions. From popular searches like “Why is my poop green?” to more personal issues like “Is divorce contagious?”, “Do I have adult ADHD?” or “Do I need filler or a facelift?” no subject is too small or too serious. With their signature chemistry and openness, Bill and Giuliana will be joined by trusted experts, notable guests and celebrity friends, ensuring every conversation is honest, insightful and entertaining. The podcast also marks a natural evolution for Bill and Giuliana, who first invited audiences into their lives on their hit reality series, “Giuliana and Bill,” which ran for seven seasons on E!. While the show documented their personal journey in real time, the podcast expands on that connection, offering a more candid and in-depth space to explore the conversations they’ve been having for years. “We’ve spent years having these kinds of conversations at our own kitchen table, and we realized everyone is asking the same questions; they’re just not always saying them out loud,” said Bill and Giuliana Rancic. “This podcast is about creating a space where every topic can be explored openly. We want people to laugh with us, learn with us, and feel a little less alone in whatever they’re going through.” Giuliana Rancic, an Emmy Award-winning journalist, entrepreneur, and New York Times bestselling author, is best known for her two decades as a leading entertainment host and red-carpet correspondent. Bill Rancic, a serial entrepreneur, motivational speaker, New York Times bestselling author, and the first-ever winner of NBC’s “The Apprentice,” has built a career spanning business, media and publishing. Together, they co-own the RPM Restaurant Group, which includes seven restaurants and growing consumer brands such as Giuliana Prosecco and the newly launched Mama Depandi’s pasta line. “ Bill and Giuliana: The Podcast ” is distributed by iHeartPodcasts as part of its Popworthy slate, where trending culture and nostalgia intersect.
