NEW YORK – May 8, 2026 – iHeartMedia highlighted new music and artists alike recently with special programming, including new World Premiere debuts and more. On May 8, Farruko’s “YAPAQUE” ft. Greeicy & Steve Aoki had a World Premiere debut across all iHeartRadio Spanish Contemporary stations while Kenny Chesney’s “Carry On” had a World Premiere debut across all iHeartCountry stations. Last week, iHeartRadio stations aired the World Premiere debut of Madonna and Sabrina Carpenter’s new collaboration "Bring Your Love" on April 30 across all iHeartRadio CHR and Hot AC, and Kacey Musgraves’ “Loneliest Girl” had a World Premiere debut on April 30 across all iHeartCountry stations.
